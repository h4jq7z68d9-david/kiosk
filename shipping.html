shipping & returns

Shipping

All prints are giclée prints, signed and dated, matted and ready to frame. Orders ship within 5–7 business days via USPS or UPS. You'll receive a tracking number by email once your order has shipped.

We ship within the United States. For international inquiries, please get in touch directly.

Returns

If you're not satisfied with your print, returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery. To initiate a return, please contact me with your order details.

Items must be returned in their original condition and packaging. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer. Refunds are issued to the original payment method once the return is received and inspected.

Damaged or defective prints will be replaced or refunded at no additional cost — please include a photo of the damage when you reach out.

Contact

Questions about an order or anything else — don't hesitate to reach out.
